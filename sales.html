Sales Guide Playbook

1. What You're Selling

Reporting automation

Dashboard development

Pipeline buildouts outside of Netsuite

2. Where Your Clients Come From
  • Upwork: Small scope. Low-budget majority. High competition.
  • Reddit: Comment a solution, and add a small offer for $49.
  • LinkedIn: Long-term inbound + warm leads.
  • Warm Network: Anyone you've worked with or supported before.
  • Outbound: Cold email + DM + brand lists (Amazon, Expo West, Shopify stores).
3. Upwork Prospecting

Filters to save:

  • Google Sheets: Intermediate + Expert, US Only, Less than 10 Proposals.
  • Netsuite: Intermediate + Expert, US Only, Less than 10 Proposals.
  • Dashboard: Intermediate + Expert, US Only, Less than 10 Proposals.
  • Automation: Intermediate + Expert, US Only, Less than 10 Proposals.
  • Domo: Intermediate + Expert, US Only, Less than 10 Proposals.
  • Excel: Intermediate + Expert, US Only, Less than 10 Proposals.

Daily routine:

  1. Check recent postings, and submit proposals.
  2. Submitted Proposals Check daily.
  3. Messages Check daily.

Proposal Scripts:

Hi there,

I can automate this for you using ... I will build this in a way that’s fast, clean, and maintenance-free. I guarantee the solution will work exactly as needed, or I’ll fix it at no additional cost.

I can start right away, just let me know.
4. LinkedIn Prospecting

Ideal clients: Ecommerce, CPG, wholesale brands, NetSuite users, 15–200 employees.

Daily activities:

  • Connect with 10 prospects.
  • Comment on 5 posts.
  • Send 3–5 DMs.

DM Scripts:

Quick question — is reporting still manual for your team or already automated?

I work with NetSuite brands a lot — how happy are you with your reporting?

If you ever want a “Reporting Audit,” I can show you the 3 areas where teams
usually lose 10–20 hours/week.
5. Cold Email Prospecting

Great places to find leads:

  • Amazon top sellers
  • Shopify stores with many apps
  • Expo West exhibitor list
  • CPG fundraising announcements

Email Scripts:

Subject: Quick question

Most teams lose 10–30 hours/week to manual reporting.

I automate all of it so dashboards update themselves.

Want me to show you a free sample?
6. How to Qualify a Lead

A lead is qualified if they say YES to any:

  • Is reporting still manual?
  • Do numbers break across systems?
  • Would automated dashboards help decision-making?
7. Discovery Call Script 
“Before I explain what I do, walk me through how reporting works at your company.”

Ask:
• Which reports take the most time?
• Where do numbers mismatch?
• What dashboards do you wish existed?

Then show:
• 1 clean dashboard
• 1 automation example
• NetSuite summary

Close:
“I can automate all of this for you. Want me to send a proposal?”
8. Proposal Framework
  • Pain points
  • Deliverables
  • Timeline
  • Pricing
  • ROI (hours saved)

Price Ranges:

  • $1,000–$2,500 small dashboards
  • $3,000–$7,500 multi dashboards
  • $8,000–$20,000 full rebuilds
  • $2,000–$4,000/mo retainer
9. Follow-Up System

If cold:

  • Day 1: bump
  • Day 3: send dashboard example
  • Day 7: send Loom video
  • Day 14: closing file message

If warm:

  • Send 1–2 quick wins
  • Offer reporting audit
  • Send preview dashboard
