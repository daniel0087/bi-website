Problems I Solve

  • Your data comes from multiple places and nothing matches.
    I map every source (NetSuite, Amazon, 3PL, retail portals) and build a clean automated pipeline so your numbers always align.
  • Your team spends hours every week building the same reports.
    I automate your most painful workflows—sales, inventory, forecasting, ATP, reconciliation—saving hours every week.
  • Your leaders can’t get the metrics they need to make decisions.
    I build simple dashboards with real KPIs so your team can act instantly without hunting for data.
  • Your current reports break, don’t refresh, or produce different numbers every run.
    I fix the logic, normalize SKUs, and ensure consistent, trusted reporting across every system.
  • You don’t trust the data in NetSuite or it doesn’t show what you need.
    I create clean saved searches, scheduled exports, and multi-layer summaries that NetSuite can’t do natively.
  • Your reporting frequency (daily, weekly, monthly) depends on manual work.
    I automate all delivery—emails, dashboards, Sheets, BigQuery—so reports update without human effort.
  • Your forecasting and replenishment processes break at scale.
    I build automated forecasting tools for Amazon, wholesale, retail, and in-house demand planning.
  • Your team wastes time reconciling numbers between Amazon, NetSuite, and 3PLs.
    I create a single source of truth so sales, inventory, payouts, and operations always match.
  • Your team wants automation, but your systems aren’t connected.
    I connect NetSuite ↔ Sheets ↔ BigQuery ↔ Domo so your entire business runs on dependable data flows.
  • You don’t know the real cost of your current reporting process.
    I quantify hours lost, errors, and delays—then replace them with a fully automated reporting engine.
